## What is Tazapay's Payment Gateway API?

The Tazapay APIs allow you to collect, hold and release payments for cross-border transactions from multiple countries.

## Get Started

To get your live mode API keys and also gain access to payment links, please follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step>
  Sign up to [Tazapay's dashboard](https://dashboard.tazapay.com/)
  </Step>
  <Step>
  Verify your email address from your inbox.
  </Step>
  <Step>
  The next step is to fill the KYB form so that Tazapay can learn more about you and your business. You can do so by clicking on `Complete Now` on the homepage
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/docs/81fb1db-Screenshot_2023-08-14_at_2.23.44_PM.png" />
  </Frame>
  <br/>
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/docs/7e80f90-Screenshot_2023-08-14_at_4.47.35_PM.png" />
  </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
  After Tazapay receives your KYB application, it takes Tazapay 2-3 business days to approve your account.
  </Step>
  <Step>
  After your account is approved, you can fetch your API keys by navigating to `Settings` --> `API Keys & Developer Docs`. These API keys will help you access Tazapay API.
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/docs/0d063cb-image.png" />
  </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  ### You can follow the same steps on [sandbox](https://dashboard-sandbox.tazapay.com/) if you need to test API keys for integration.

  * Sandbox environment keys will differ from production environment keys.
  * Sign up with Google does **NOT** work on sandbox.
</Info>
